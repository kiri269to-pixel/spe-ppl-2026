About The Event

Where Pakistan's Energy
Future Is Shaped

The SPE–PPL Technical Symposium & Energy Summit 2026 is a premier gathering organized by the SPE Mehran Student Chapter in association with Pakistan Petroleum Limited — bringing together students, academics, and industry leaders from across Pakistan.

Across two intensive days, participants will explore cutting-edge developments in petroleum engineering, renewable energy integration, digital transformation, and career pathways in the energy sector.

Featuring keynote addresses, technical sessions by industry experts, panel discussions, and competitive events — this is the defining event for aspiring petroleum engineers in Pakistan.

"Powering Pakistan's future demands innovation, collaboration, and the brightest minds in energy."
